The Boiling Isles

The Boiling Isles is one of the archipelagos in the demon realm where the majority of the show takes place, being the home of the titular Owl House and Hexside School of Magic and Demonics. The Boiling Isles is a land where magic exists in everything from the ground itself to the air.

The Titan

The Titan is the being that makes up the Boiling Isles, with his corpse being the main landmass. The Titan is older than everything that exists in the isles and was an impossibly powerful being whose residual magic gave all the demons and witches in the isles life and magic.

Magic

Magic in the Boiling Isles is a natural energy that all witches and demons can naturally control and shape to do wonderful things from shaping the ground around them into castles or making illusions so real they do physical harm or even just simply blowing things up.

Covens

Covens are a system put in place by Belos to seal certain types of magic in individuals because it is supposedly unnatural to use all of the Titan's magic and so they should be limited. There are ten main Covens each of which seals all but one type of magic (excluding the Emperor's Coven):

  • The Emperor's Coven
  • The Abomination Coven
  • The Bard Coven
  • The Beast Keeping Coven
  • The Construction Coven
  • The Healing Coven
  • The Illusionist Coven
  • The Oracle Coven
  • The Plant Coven
  • The Potions Coven
