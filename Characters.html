The Main Cast

Luz Noceda

Luz Noceda is the main character of The Owl House.
She is a bubbly optimistic and self-proclaimed weirdo
that tries her best to make everyone happy even when
she goes a bit too far or messes up she is always earnestly
trying her best. Originally from the human realm after
having issues in school she runs away to the demon
realm ending up in a place called the Boiling Isles where
she meets and befriends fellow outcasts and "weirdos" and
resolves to become a real witch and learn magic from...

Eda the Owl Lady

Eda is the first person Luz meets when she enters
the Boiling Isles. Eda is a carefree witch that believes
in freedom above all else rebeling against the Coven
system to keep all her magic and be the most powerful
witch on the Boiling Isles. She is a bit aloof and doesn't
always show that she cares but she's a loving
mother-figure to both Luz and...

King of Demons

King is a little guy with a big attitude. The self-proclaimed
king of demons, no one recognizes his authority and they
all continue to treat him as a baby which annoys him to
no end. Despite his attitude problems he, like the others,
has a kind heart and just wants to keep his family
together and make sure they're safe and happy

The Supporting Cast

Willow Park

Willow Park is a student from Hexside School of Magic and Demonics in the plant track and is one of the most powerful plant witches in the Boiling Isles. She is the first and one of the best friends that Luz makes in her time at Hexside. She wants to protect everyone she cares about and will do whatever she needs to to do it

Gus Porter

Gus is an illusion witch at Hexside and another of Luz's friends. He is an enthusiast for human culture with a large collection of human things though he doesn't quite know what they are. He is a prodigy, being the youngest in the school and that puts a lot of pressure on him but he manages to push through it all and protect his friends

Amity Blight

Amity is a rich kid that has always been the best at everything she does and when Luz shows her up she tries everything to prove that she's better but as Luz keeps trying to prove herself as a witch Amity starts to show genuine care for not only Luz but the rest of Hexside. Though it takes a while to find it, Amity's heart is big and full of love and she will do anything to protect those around her

The Villain

Emperor Belos

Emperor Belos is the ruler of the Boiling Isles seeking to control all magic
by sealing people's magic so they can only use one type of magic. He rules
through fear with an iron fist capturing anybody who does anything not
in line with his vision for the isles. He has ruled for a long time and says
he can speak to the Titan and is enacting his will and he will do everything
in his power to destroy anything that gets in his way
